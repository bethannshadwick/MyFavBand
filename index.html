Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band from Sheffield. Group members Alex Turner, Jamie Cook, Nick O'Malley, and Matt Helders started the band in 2002. They have released six studio albums and one live album since their start. Their debut album is the fastest-selling debut album by a background in United Kingdom history. Their newest album released in 2018, and is called "Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino". Go check it out!
